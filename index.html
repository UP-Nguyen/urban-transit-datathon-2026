Why Does Urban Rail Cost So Much?

An Urban Transit rail project, built with love using data from https://ultraviolet.library.nyu.edu/records/9wnjp-kez15.
This visualization compares 884 rail projects across 59 countries and shows that rail costs rise with tunneling and project complexity, but some countries still build much more cheaply than others. Hover over a country to see its rail-building profile. Click to drill down into cities and projects. The map shows median cost per kilometer in 2023 dollars, while the scatterplot reveals how project length, stations, and alignment interact.

Project anatomy

Each dot is a project. Larger dots have more stations. Colors show the dominant alignment by kilometers built.

How to read this

This app uses Cost_km_2023_dollars from https://ultraviolet.library.nyu.edu/records/9wnjp-kez15 and derives a project's dominant alignment from whichever is largest among tunnel, elevated, and at-grade kilometers. Country colors summarize project-level values, so they should be read as a comparative overview rather than a definitive national accounting.
